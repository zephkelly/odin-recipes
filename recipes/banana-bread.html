Banana Bread

Description:

This really is the best banana bread recipe, period. You can mix everything in one bowl, you can vary the amount of sugar or bananas. And the secret to its great flavor? Melted butter.

The beauty of this banana bread recipe is you don't need a fancy mixer! A mixing bowl, a fork to whisk the eggs, and a sturdy spoon to mix the batter are all you need. You can toss in a cup of chopped nuts, raisins, or chocolate chips if you want, or put the batter into muffin tins and make banana nut muffins instead. You can even go a step further and make chocolate banana bread.

Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 medium very ripe bananas, peeled
  • 1/3 cup butter, unsalted or salted, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare the pan
  2. Mash the bananas and add the butter
  3. Mix in the remaining ingredients
  4. Bake the bread
  5. Cool and serve
