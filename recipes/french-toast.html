French Toast

Description:

French toast will always be a staple for leisurely weekend breakfasts along with my favourite fluffy Pancakes and Pancakes bursting with Blueberries. So skip the bistro queue and make brunch at home this weekend!

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 2-3 tbsp butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Strawberries sliced
  • Maple syrup

Steps:

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon powder, and vanilla extract in a bowl
  2. Melt butter in non-stick pan
  3. Place bread briefly in bread mixture, coating both sides
  4. Cook on pan for 2-3 minutes each side or until golden
  5. Server with butter and maple syrup
